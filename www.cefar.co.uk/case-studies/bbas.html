The Brief

BBAS were using various off-the-shelf applications to run their business. They wanted to engage with a company to provide a bespoke solution tailored to their requirements.

We recommended a scalable solution for their website, ecommerce product offering and a complete end-to-end management system which allowed them to manage their whole business from website enquiries through to billing and support field salesforce.

The Solution

The website was designed with a simplicity that is attractive yet functional, showcasing all their products in a way that aligns with the brand identity. The website displays their product and offerings in a stylish way. A carousel of images was incorporated on the Home Page so that users would be welcomed to the website by being given a taster of BBAS offers, emphasising the character of the website and culture of the company.

A big part of this project was to initially integrate the website with various external management system's they used. This meant the website would update in real-time when changes are made through the off-site salesforce.

The second phase of the website project was to adapt the website to an ecommerce website where we expanded on the user journey and engagement with strong functionality providing website visitors with the best experience of the BBAS brand's product and long term offering.

A sophisticated management system with dashboards, workflow and reports is currently being developed.
