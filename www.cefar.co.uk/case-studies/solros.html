Solros Group

We worked closely with the client to define their brand strategy and then put it into action. We created a strong, distinctive and professional look and feel that began with the logo and then evolved. Amongst the collaterals that we went on to design and execute were the overarching brand guidelines, the website, billboards and even site safety and information posters. The result: a consistent suite of material that positions Solros as experts in the field.

Background

Solros Group is a fast-growing business operating in the construction sector. Following a period of rapid growth, the business directors felt it was time to firmly establish the brand with an identity that reflects their standing in the industry.
