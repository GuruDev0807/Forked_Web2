Yorkshire's Finest

As a company that is proud of its Yorkshire roots, Springfield provide countrywide apprenticeship opportunities as well as online training courses covering everything from food safety to working at heights. The organisation is on a mission to "change the world through educating one person at a time" and when they came to us the brief was to express this with our branding solution.

So we were tasked with devising a compelling solution that would capture the essence of the brand and be as appealing and inspiring to potential apprentices and employers alike.
