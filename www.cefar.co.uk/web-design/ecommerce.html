Do you run a retail business that's doing well and you're wondering how to expand sales globally?

At Cefar we design and build interactive ecommerce websites. How our ECommerce websites make you sell more?

We work with both traditional 'brick and mortar' business and online only operations. As a digital agency, we're comfortable building across a range of platforms. From Magento to WordPress, Woocommerce and everything in between. Find out more about our approach to ecommerce below, or get in touch with us to discuss your project.

Designed to Sell

We don't just build sites that look pretty. We build our ecommerce stores with sales in mind. Using our marketing experience, we use conversion rate optimisation techniques to design an e-commerce site that converts more customers, making you more money.

Quick Loading Times

Did you know?

40% of users will abandon a site that takes more than 2 seconds to load, so we build our systems looking for the best loading times.

Responsive & Optimised

The mobile revolution has happened, and mobile users will make up a majority of your customers. Cefar's responsive ecommerce websites remain beautiful and usable on any size of screen so you don't miss out on vital sales.

Marketing in Mind

We don't just stop at building your ecommerce site. Our experienced marketing team can help drive new users to your website. Using a data-driven approach, we can advise you on the best channels to reach your target audience ensuring your website starts making money from launch.

Track Your Performance

You want to know how well your new website is performing, and where to make improvements in future. Cefar will help you set up automated reporting that's easy to understand and shows you which areas of the site are strongest, and which need more work.

Build a Strong Brand

Brand is a vital part of creating loyal customers and earning new ones. Cefar have a lot of experience with designing websites that focus on enhancing your brand as well as selling your products.

Knowledge is power. When Cefar design your website, we build the knowledge in FOR YOU.

We build ecommerce websites using the latest payment processing system providing you with a payment gateway solution that takes care of everything 24/7. From product management to payment processing, we will ensure that your ecommerce website offers a smooth user journey and an easy to manage back office, admin panel.
